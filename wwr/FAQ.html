FAQ

Are there any age restrictions for rafting?

Dry Oar is proud to accomadate anyone between the ages of 12 and 65. Children under the age of 18 are required to be accompanied by an adult (21+) at all times. Adults over the age of 65 are not permitted due to government reulations. All attendees are required to wear life jackets at all times.

When is the best time to go rafting?

The best time to come raft with us is between June and October, when the snow is melted and the water isn't too cold.

How must does it cost to raft with Dry Oar?

We have several different options based on your skill level and desired level of difficulty.

- 25 mile trip - $20 admission/person

- 10 mile trip - $10 admission/person

- 5 mile trip - $7 admission/person

- 2 mile trip - $5 admission/person

- Raft Rental(8 person capacity) - $100/day

- Life Jacket rental - $5/person

Are there any weight restrictions?

There are weight restrictions. Indivduals under the weight of 100lbs or over the weight 220lbs are not permitted for safety reasons.

Is there phone service out on the river?

Most people have service while at the check-in office but shortly after being bused off to the location where you'll get in the water, there will be no service.
